## General Questions

**Q: How do I book a service appointment?**
A: Appointments can be booked via the Maruti Suzuki "Service Support App", by calling
the service centre helpline, or through a walk-in. Online booking guarantees a service
bay slot within 24-48 hours; walk-ins may face same-day waiting depending on load.

**Q: What is included in a periodic service?**
A: Periodic (scheduled) service includes engine oil and filter replacement, air filter
check, brake inspection, coolant top-up, battery health check, tyre rotation, and a
21-point vehicle health checkup. Exact inclusions vary by service interval (see table
below).

**Q: Do I need to service my car at the same centre every time?**
A: No. Maruti Suzuki service history is centrally logged via the chassis/VIN number,
so any authorized service centre across India can access your car's service records.

## Common Issues Brought to the Service Centre

1. **AC not cooling properly** — usually gas top-up (Rs. 1,200-1,800) or compressor
   clutch issue (Rs. 3,500+ depending on model).
2. **Battery discharge complaints** — free health check; replacement battery (35Ah-60Ah)
   priced Rs. 4,500-8,000 depending on model, with 1-year warranty (Amaron/Exide OEM).
3. **Clutch/gearbox noise (manual transmission)** — inspected under free service if
   within warranty period; clutch plate replacement typically Rs. 4,000-6,000 for labor
   + parts.
4. **Infotainment/SmartPlay touchscreen freeze** — software update at service centre,
   usually resolved free of cost within warranty.
5. **Suspension noise over speed bumps** — most common on Swift and Baleno with
   aftermarket lowering; original suspension bushing replacement Rs. 2,000-3,500.

## Warranty Policy Summary

- Standard new car warranty: 2 years / 40,000 km (whichever is earlier)
- Extended warranty (optional, paid): up to 5 years / 1,00,000 km
- Warranty does NOT cover: consumables (wiper blades, brake pads beyond first
  replacement), damage from accidents, unauthorized modifications, or use of
  non-genuine spare parts
- Roadside Assistance (RSA) is complimentary for the first year on all new vehicles,
  and can be renewed annually for approx. Rs. 1,500-2,000

## Pickup & Drop / Doorstep Service

Jaipur City Motors offers a free pickup-and-drop facility within 10 km of the service
centre for scheduled services. Doorstep minor repairs (battery jump-start, minor
diagnostics) are available via the "Maruti Suzuki Express Service" van, dispatched
within city limits for an additional Rs. 300 convenience fee.
